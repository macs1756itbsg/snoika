What is Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)?

GEO — or Generative Engine Optimization — is the practice of optimizing your brand and content so that AI language models like ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity cite and recommend you in their generated answers.

Traditional SEO focuses on ranking in Google's blue links. GEO goes further: it ensures your brand appears in the AI-generated summaries, answers, and recommendations that are now reshaping how people discover products and services.
